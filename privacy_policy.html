Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Introduction

Inburgering Mate ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Dutch language learning application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app is designed to work entirely offline and does not require user accounts, registration, or any personal data.

Data Storage

All data related to your learning progress (such as completed lessons and vocabulary progress) is stored locally on your device only. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect any information.

In-App Purchases

If you make an in-app purchase to unlock premium features, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Play Store). We do not have access to your payment information. Please refer to Apple's or Google's privacy policies for information about how they handle payment data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
